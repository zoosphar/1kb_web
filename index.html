Minimalistic & Efficient

Reduce things to their essential elements:
Our minimalist design philosophy uses only the bare essentials to create simple, uncluttered spaces. Characterized by simplicity, clean lines, and a monochromatic palette with occasional color accents, our designs often feature open floor plans, ample light, and functional furniture. We emphasize the shape, color, and texture of a few key elements to craft serene and elegant environments.

About Us

Welcome to 1kb.homes, where every space tells a story.
Founded by Ar. Kashish Bhutani, a Brick School of Architecture alumna, our studio brings minimalistic design philosophy to life. With experience from Bangalore and Jaipur, Kashish crafts spaces that blend simplicity with elegance.

Our mission is creating homes that reflect your unique personality while maximizing functionality. Whether it's a compact urban apartment or a spacious retreat, we infuse each project with warmth and innovative space-saving solutions. As a young, dynamic studio, we're passionate about pushing boundaries in Architecture and Interior Design, bringing your vision to life with meticulous attention to detail.
